# Responsible AI Checklist (Demo)
- PII redaction in outputs/logs
- Prompt-injection test cases included in evals
- Human review for low-confidence answers
- Audit logging & retention policy
- IAM/RBAC least privilege; secrets via env
- Encryption in transit/at rest
- **Retention:** Logs retained for 90 days for audits, then purged.
- **Citation Requirement:** Answers that summarize policy must include at least one citation.
